Three Sneaky Storage Solutions For Small Spaces

Storage idea #1: Keep what you love and use, get rid of the rest:While not necessarily a “clever” storage idea for small rooms, the first, and perhaps most important thing to do before finding any storage solutions for small spaces is to do an audit of the stuff you’re storing. We know it sounds obvious, but with less stuff to store, there will be fewer storage solutions to find.
Storage idea #2: Think of your small apartment as a city:For inspiration, let’s think about cities. More specifically, let’s think about how cities fit more people, homes, and businesses into a limited footprint: by building up, not out.
